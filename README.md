# 📊 Sales Analytics Dashboard – Power BI Project

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive **Power BI Dashboard** built to analyze:

* 📈 Sales Performance Trends
* 🌍 Regional Sales Distribution
* 🍕 Pizza Sales Analysis (Size, Category, Time-Based Trends)
* 💰 Revenue & Pricing Insights

The dashboard provides business insights that help in decision-making related to product performance, regional sales, and customer purchasing behavior.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze monthly sales trends
* Compare regional sales performance
* Identify best-selling product categories
* Understand customer ordering patterns by time
* Evaluate revenue contribution by pizza size

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1️⃣ Sales Overview Dashboard

* **Sum of Sales by Month-Year**

  * Tracks sales trend over time
  * Helps identify seasonal patterns and decline/increase periods

* **Sales by Region**

  * West, East, Central, South comparison
  * Identifies top-performing and underperforming regions

* **Sales by Category**

  * Technology
  * Furniture
  * Office Supplies

---

### 2️⃣ Pizza Sales Analytics Dashboard

* **Total Sales by Pizza Size**

  * L (Largest contributor ~45%)
  * M
  * S
  * XL
  * XXL

* **Top 5 Pizzas by Category**

  * Chicken
  * Classic
  * Supreme
  * Veggie

* **Orders by Hour**

  * Peak ordering hours identified
  * Useful for staffing and inventory planning

* **Total Price vs Unit Price Analysis**

  * Revenue vs pricing relationship visualization

---

## 📂 Dataset Information

The dataset contains:

* Order ID
* Order Time
* Pizza Category
* Pizza Size
* Quantity
* Unit Price
* Total Price
* Region
* Sales Amount

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies Used

* **Power BI Desktop**
* Data Modeling
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Data Cleaning & Transformation (Power Query)
* Interactive Visualizations

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* 📌 Large (L) size pizzas generate the highest revenue.
* 📌 West region contributes the highest sales.
* 📌 Peak orders occur during afternoon hours.
* 📌 Technology category leads in overall sales (from retail dataset).

---

## 💡 Business Use Cases

This dashboard can help businesses:

* Improve inventory planning
* Optimize staffing during peak hours
* Focus marketing on high-performing categories
* Improve regional sales strategies
